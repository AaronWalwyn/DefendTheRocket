Weapons
===================

Blaster 3000X - Pistol
The choice weapon of the Royal Space Fleet. It won't be much use in a war but it'll get you out of a jam.


Carmack Rifle - Bolt Action Rifle
The Carmack Rifle makes use of good ol' fashioned gun powder, it'll get the job done one way or another.


M24 Assault Rifle - Automatic Rifle
The M24 Assault Rifle makes killing aliens fun and easy. Simply hold down the trigger and watch them die.


Tesla Rifle - Bolt Action Rifle
This weapon deals damage like nothing you've ever seen, unfortunatly it takes a while to actually shoot.


Skull Crusher - Automatic Rifle
Created from what looks like smashing two guns together; the 'Skull Crusher' still manages to deliver deadly damage.


Scar - Bolt Action Rifle
The Scar Rifle is aptly named as it looks like it's survived several wars, but apparently it packs a punch.


Eden - Automatic Rifle
'Powerful beyond measure...' is engraved on the side of this weapon, as you look at it it gives off a faint glow.


DSX0-01 - Pistol
The DSX0-01 was a prototype weapon stolen from Rhea military, you don't know what it does but it looks badass.


Avenger - Automatic Rifle
Choice weapon of the Reflex gang, the Avenger is known for it fast fire rate and high damage.


Proton Rifle - Bolt Action Rifle
At the forefront of weapon tech, the Proton Rifle allows you to kill enemies in one, however not very quickly.


Dominator - Automatic Rifle
This fusion gun harness plasma energy to destroy your enemies, removing them from the battlefield.



Ship Upgrades
===================



Field Booster I, II, III
Increases forcefield strenght


Crew Quaters I, II, III
Increases maximum number of crew


Ship Weapons I, II
Adds an additional gun to the ship increasing rate of mercenary fire.


Laboratory
Increases the rate of research.


Refinery
Increases rate of fuel collection.
