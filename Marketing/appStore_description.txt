You are the captain upon the HMS Soverign, a scout ships tasked with exploring distant solar system and establishing contact with alien races. Completely by accident your ships fell through a wormhole and you have ended up in a aggressive star system.

Trapped in a hostile system you must defend your rocket and crew against hoards of aliens. Hire mercenaries, engineers and scientists to help you fight, repair and even upgrade your rocket.

Features: 
- 6 Unique planets
- 30 Enemy types
- 21 Blueprints
- 11 Weapons
- 10 Ship upgrades
